Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Bob University app from the App Store or Google Play. Tap "Sign Up" and enter your email address or sign in with Apple/Google. You'll be guided through a quick onboarding process to personalize your learning experience.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes access to introductory video content, basic tutorials, and the ability to browse the community. Premium features like full course access, certifications, and the stylist directory require a subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

For iOS (App Store): Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Bob University → Cancel Subscription.

For Android (Google Play): Open Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions → Bob University → Cancel.

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Sign in with the same account you used to purchase. Go to Profile → Settings → Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be automatically restored if it's still active.

Can I download videos for offline viewing?

Currently, videos require an internet connection to stream. We're working on offline viewing for a future update.

How do certifications work?

Certifications require completing specific course modules and demonstrating your skills. Once you've met the requirements, you can apply for certification through the app. Our team will review your submission and issue your certificate upon approval.

How do I get listed in the Stylist Directory?

Premium members can create a public profile in the Stylist Directory. Go to Profile → Stylist Directory → Edit Profile to add your location, specialties, photos, and contact information. Your listing will be visible to potential clients searching in your area.

What's the difference between Individual and Salon plans?

The Individual plan is perfect for solo stylists and includes full access to all courses, certifications, directory listing, and community features.

The Salon plan includes everything in Individual plus team management features, allowing salon owners to provide access to multiple team members under one subscription.

I'm having technical issues with the app. What should I do?

Try these steps first:

  1. Force close and reopen the app
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Update to the latest version from the App Store/Google Play
  4. Restart your device

If the issue persists, email us at support@bobuniversity.com with a description of the problem and your device type.
