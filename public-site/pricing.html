Pricing Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. Note: If you're listed in the Stylist Directory, your listing will be removed when your subscription ends.
What's the difference between Signature and Studio?

Signature gives you full access to the course library, monthly live workshops, and community features.

Studio adds direct weekly access to Ray through "Ask Ray" live sessions, exclusive business/pricing content (Demand), studio-only replays, and reserved seats at live events. It's for stylists who want more proximity to Ray.

How does certification work?

Certification is a $297 one-time purchase available to Signature and Studio members. It's a 4-week live intensive with Ray, offered quarterly.

You'll attend 90-minute live sessions each Monday, work through the certification syllabus, and test out at the end. Upon passing, you earn your certificate, an in-app badge, and eligibility to be listed in the Stylist Directory.

Do I need a subscription to stay in the directory?
Yes. To maintain your listing in the Certified Stylist Directory, you need an active Signature, Studio, or Salon subscription. This ensures all listed stylists have access to continuing education and stay current with Ray's methods.
Can I add more than 5 people to a Salon plan?
Yes! The Salon plan includes 5 seats, and you can add additional seats at a discounted per-seat rate. Contact us for details on larger teams.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! New users can explore free content without a subscription. When you're ready to upgrade, your first 7 days of premium access are free.
